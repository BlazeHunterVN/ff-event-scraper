Changes to JJK Weapons

Title: Changes to JJK Weapons

Start: 15 Jan 2026 12:00 AM

End: 13 Feb 2026 11:59 PM

Details:
Dear Survivors,

We’ve received feedback that the Desert Eagle - Recharge starts with only 7 rounds and pistol ammo is difficult to find in BR. We've made the following adjustments: you can now get 30 free pistol ammo in the Vending Machine for your Desert Eagle - Recharge.

Additionally, we’re still seeing feedback that the Kugisaki Hammer remains too strong and too easy to obtain. To address this, we’re increasing the price of the Hammer:

Hammer I: 50 (no change)
Hammer II: 50 → 100
Hammer III: 100 → 200

This change is live now.

Thank you for your continued feedback and support, and we wish you Happy Booyah!
